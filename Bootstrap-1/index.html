YOU GOTTA NOURISH TO FLOURISH

Macronutrients are the nutrients in food that your body needs in large amounts: carbohydrates, fat, and protein. These nutrients give you energy and keep your body systems functioning.

PROTEIN

< MAIN BUILDING BLOCK >

Needed for more than just muscle growth

Made up of many different amino acids

Adults need to eat 0.8 grams protein per kilogram of weight

CARBS

< SOURCE OF ENERGY >

Supply your body with glucose, which your cells use as fuel

Healthiest sources of carbohydrates—unprocessed or minimally processed starches

Unhealthier sources of carbohydrates include breads, pasta, pastries, sodas

FAT

< BACKBONE TO HORMONES >

Complex molecules composed of fatty acids and glycerol

Body needs fats for growth and energy

Slowest source of energy but the most energy-efficient form of food
